5-Phase Mastery Pathway
🌱
Phase 1 — Cloud Foundations
Months 1–2
Build a rock-solid mental model of AWS global infrastructure, core services, billing models, and the Shared Responsibility Model. This phase eliminates confusion and establishes the conceptual scaffolding for everything ahead.
AWS Regions & AZsIAM Basics EC2 FundamentalsS3 Storage VPC FundamentalsAWS CLI / Console Pricing & TCOShared Responsibility Model
🎯 AWS Cloud Practitioner (CLF-C02)
🔧
Phase 2 — Associate Architecture
Months 3–5
Master production-grade services and their interconnections. Learn design patterns for high availability, fault tolerance, scalability, and cost optimization. This is the foundation of your architectural thinking.
VPC Advanced (Peering, Transit GW) Auto Scaling & ELBRDS & DynamoDB CloudFront & Route53SQS · SNS · EventBridge CloudWatch & CloudTrailLambda & API Gateway Elastic Beanstalk · ECS
🎯 AWS Solutions Architect Associate (SAA-C03)

Phase 3 — Professional Architecture
Months 6–10
Elevate to enterprise-grade design. This phase focuses on multi-account governance, complex hybrid architectures, migrations, FinOps, and the AWS Well-Architected Framework — the level expected of a senior consultant.
AWS Organizations & Control Tower AWS Landing ZoneDirect Connect & VPN CloudFormation / CDK (IaC) AWS Migration Hub & 7Rs FinOps & Cost Governance WAF · Shield · GuardDuty DR Strategies (RTO/RPO) Well-Architected Framework
🎯 AWS Solutions Architect Professional (SAP-C02)
⚡
Phase 4 — Specialization Tracks
Months 11–14
Choose 1–2 specialization tracks that align with your strategic advisory positioning. Given your 20+ years of infrastructure expertise, the Security and AI/ML tracks command the highest consulting value in the Saudi market.
🤖 AI/ML · SageMaker 🔒 Security Specialty 📦 Data Analytics 🌐 Advanced Networking 🛠️ DevOps Professional ☁️ Database Specialty
🎯 1–2 AWS Specialty Certifications
🏆
Phase 5 — Strategic Consulting Mastery
Months 15–18
Transition from technical architect to trusted strategic advisor. This phase is about governance leadership, executive communication, and building the advisory identity that distinguishes you from a technician.
AWS Well-Architected Reviews Cloud Governance Frameworks Executive Briefing & Proposals TCO & Business Case Design Cloud Center of Excellence (CCoE) Risk & Compliance Advisory AWS Partner Network (APN) Vision 2030 Cloud Alignment
🎯 APN Consulting Partner · AWS Ambassador Track
